NECESSARY INFORMATION FOR THE GAME:

-Create int variables x1y1c, x1y2c, x1y3c, etc until 
x10y10p and x10y10p (grid for player and computer)
-int variables xstartac, xendac, ystartac, yendac,
 xstartbs, ystartbs, etc (start and end coordinates for aircraft carrier, battleship, etc)

-error conditions: user cannot enter numbers less
 than 1 or greater than 10 for these coordinates, 
or create diagonal ships, or choose 
coordinates that exceed the length of the board or 
exceeds the length of the specified ship.
